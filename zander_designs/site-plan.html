Overview

Purpose/Elevator Pitch

Are you starting a new business, or are you self-employed? Would you like to reach more people through a web page but do not know how to go about it? Maybe you have a page that is not working well, and you have spent too much of your precious time on it. Depending on your needs, I will design and create a user-friendly web page and give you suggestions on promoting your business through a web page.

Audience

My audience consists of Entrepreneurs, young and old, and corportions of all sizes. The problems with generalization are that that would consist of anyone interested in getting into the world wide web and neglecting the pinpointed area for which one would be looking for a designer. Therefore, I would need to show versatility. Many children nowadays, have been successful in creating thier own companies. They may want to take their business to another level by adding an online presence. Then there are a variety of companies that may need to revise the current design or begin creating a web page. these are some scenarios of the targeted audience.

Designer defined
Merriam-Webster Dictionary defines a designer as "one who creates and often executes plans for a project or structure" and "artificially synthesized or modified to meet a specific need or requirement or serve a particular function".

Developer defined
To define a developer, one must look at the root word and see its meaning. Merriam-Webster Dictionary defines develop as, "start to exist, experience, or possess".
Merriam-Webster Dictionary defines a Developer as "a person of thing that develope somthing".

What I do
Not all companies' web page needs are created equal. I will listen to your needs and give you ideas to make your page beautiful and user-friendly, and that works.

What I will design
If you do not have an internet presence, I will create one for you. The creating process may include any of the following Depending on your specific need/needs.

Designing a logo
Designing a logo or graphic design is not just about art. It is about communicating to someone a message in a short glance. It takes necessary research of the company, the consumers, and the competition.

Creating Content
I will work with you in choosing content for your page, for example, the graphics, videos, photos, links to social media, text, and more that you want on your page. I will guide you in having media that will keep the viewer' attention and interest in your page.

Creating wireframes
As in creating logos, webpages need correct placement and design for how they will function and look. Therefore, I will make wireframes for your page/pages.

Publishing
All is great when you have a beautiful, working web page/pages, but it does no good if it is not on the web. Therefore, I will help in creating a domain name and a host.

About Me

About Me
Because I enjoy technology, designing, and creating, I enrolled in school. I am attending Brigham Young University-Idaho online for a bachelor's degree in Applied Technology. My chosen areas of focus are graphic design, web front end, and web back end.

Interests
Over the years and before enrolling in classes, I created heirloom videos for special and sometimes sad occassions. I made newsletters and instruction printouts for nonprofit organizations. I made some digitized embroidery designs, and I sew and machine embroidery. I am learning to use a drone for photography, and I enjoy it.

My Past Work
For the past 30 years, I have managed residential apartments. I learned to listen and to work with clients and with contractors. I will bring this communication skill with me as I work with clients designing and developing a web page and site for them.

Family
Most importantly, I love my family. I enjoy spending time with them celebrating, playing games, talking, working, and traveling. One of our favorite places to visit is Manhattan, New York.

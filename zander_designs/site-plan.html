Overview

Purpose/Elevator Pitch

Are you starting a new business, or are you self-employed? Would you like to reach more people through a web page but do not know how to go about it? Or maybe you have a page that is not working well, and you have spent too much of your precious time on it. That's what I do. Depending on your needs, I will design and create a user-friendly web page and give you suggestions on promoting your business through a web page.

Audience

My audience consists of Entrepreneurs, young and old, and corportions of all sizes. The problems with generalization are that that would consist of anyone interested in getting into the world wide web and neglecting the pinpointed area for which one would be looking for a designer. Therefore, I would need to show versatility. Many children nowadays, have been successful in creating thier own companies. They may want to take their business to another level by adding an online presence. Then there are a variety of companies that may need to revise the current design or begin creating a web page. these are some scenarios of the targeted audience.

Normal paragraph example

I picked Joan for my header font because it is easy to read, has serifs, and has curvature characteristics. I like how it looks with the words, 'Zander Web Design'.
I picked Roboto for my paragraph text because there are no serifs, it is easy to read, it is simple, and I like it.

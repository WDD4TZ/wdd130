What I do

Not all companies' web page needs are created equal. I will listen to your needs and give you ideas to make your page beautiful and user-friendly and that works great.


What I will design

If you do not have an internet presence, I will create one for you. The creating process may include any of the following depending on your specific need/needs.

Designing a Logo

Designing a logo or graphic design is not just about art. It is about communicating to someone a message in a short glance. It takes necessary research of the company, the consumers, and the competition.

Creating Content

I will work with you in choosing content for your page, for example, the graphics, videos, photos, links to social media, text, and more that you want on your page. I will guide you in having media that will keep the viewers' attention and interest in your page.

Creating Wireframes

As in creating logos, webpages need correct placement and design for how they will function and look. Therefore, I will make wireframes for your page/pages.

Publishing

All is great when you have a beautiful, working web page/pages, but it does no good if it is not on the web. Therefore, I will help in creating a domain main name and a host.

Font Poster

This project consisted of research, concept thumbnails, digital sketches, preliminary concepts, color variations, mockups, and a behance.net post. The project needed to include the following, size of 16" X 20", the font I chose, history/information about the font, font designer's name, samples of all the weights I had available, pricing and contact information, and a three-color minimum.

Glyph Composition

This assignment consisted of using 5-8 different characters and at least five different types of font anatomy.
Composition 1 consists of Neu Haas Grotesk bold font. I chose to spell the word SIMPLE. Do you see the word?
Composition 2 consists of Stempel Garamond bold. I chose to spell the word STRONG. Do you see this word?

A Gift Booklet

I chose a talk from a current address, at the time, from The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints general conference to create this final project. The title is 'Light Cleaveth Unto Light' by Elder Timothy J. Dyches. Some of the requirements are to use one picture of the speaker, character and paragraphe styles, page numbers, am minimum of three colors, grids, margins, gutters, etc. We were also to keep in mind who our audience would be. I chose my family as my audience. These mockups came from freepik.com as a free download.
